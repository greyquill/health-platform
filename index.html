Hospital Operating System

One platform to run the whole hospital.

Greyquill Health Platform is a multi-tenant hospital operating system: a single backend that runs clinical care, nursing, revenue cycle, pharmacy, labs, imaging, facilities, EMS, outpatient, HR and compliance for 11+ staff roles and your patients, built on open standards so it coexists with the systems you already run.

One platform, every department

From the bedside to the billing office, on one backend.

Every department works off the same patient, the same record and the same permissions model, so handoffs stop falling through the cracks between disconnected systems.

🩺 Clinical core

Patient panel, order entry, e-prescribing, clinical notes and chart summary for the doctor's full workflow.

💉 Nursing

eMAR medication pass, vitals capture, nursing notes, task lists and SBAR handover at the point of care.

💳 Revenue cycle

AR worklist, invoice detail, payment posting, denials follow-up and a payments reconciliation ledger.

💊 Pharmacy & labs

Dispense queue and pharmacy inventory; lab result entry and specimen tracking, linked to orders.

🩻 Imaging

Modality worklist with image capture and quality control for the radiography team.

🛠️ Facilities & biomed

Asset register, work-order queue, preventive-maintenance schedules and biomedical calibration tracking.

🚑 EMS & ambulance

Unit status board, dispatch and ED handoff, plus completed trip history for the crew.

🏥 Outpatient clinics

Clinic management and day reports for the coordinator, with patient appointment booking and telemed lobby.

📋 HR & credentialing

A staff credentialing vault tracking license and credential expiry, with a hospital staffing overview.

🔒 Compliance & audit

A read-only audit trail of every staff write, surfaced to the compliance officer role.

⚕️ Clinical decision support

Risk scores and safety alerts surfaced inline in the doctor's workflow.

🔁 Interoperability

FHIR R4 and HL7 v2 endpoints plus a terminology service, so coded data flows in and out cleanly.

Why hospitals choose Greyquill

Built to fit the hospital you have, not the one a vendor wishes you had.

🔗 Open standards

Built on FHIR R4 and HL7 v2, so it coexists with what you run instead of forcing a rip-and-replace.

🔑 One login, every role

A single unified staff login serves 13 roles plus the patient app; permissions come from the account, not a separate system per department.

🛡️ Audited by design

Multi-tenant isolation at the data layer and an audit trail that captures every staff write, automatically and non-blocking.

⚙️ Deploys on your infrastructure

Native deploy with no Docker required, running under pm2 or systemd on your own servers. See the platform.

📈 Phased adoption

Stand up one department at a time and grow into the rest. Phased adoption means no big-bang cutover.

✅ Verified on every deploy

A per-role smoke check logs in as each persona, exercises its worklist and runs a cross-role access check as an automated go/no-go gate.
